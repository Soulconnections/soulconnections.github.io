Darilyn/Sky has the ability to tap into information about your soul journey and current situation so that new perspective can be viewed. Her channeling often comes through in the form of very strong and descriptive metaphor allowing the receiver to see immediately and clearly the qualities and energies inherent within themselves, their circles of support and environment.

These images suggest possibilites for profound growth and healing. Her focus is not to tell you your future, or to outline the past, although conditions of each may described in detail; rather she channels information for the receiver, supporting ownership of each person's unique journey, guiding them to take the steps towards fuller responsibity, greater joy and the inward peace that comes with knowing oneself more deeply.
